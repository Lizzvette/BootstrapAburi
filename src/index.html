Trails

Nestled in the Mountains of Ghana, Aburi Botanical Gardens focus was primarily a culture experiment with indigenous crops such as cocoa, rubber plants and cola. In addition, there were also ornamental and fruit plants grown of various kinds and small field trials with cotton and spices, vanilla and pepper mainly on cardamom and nutmeg. .
